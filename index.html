COVID-19 Protocol

At Silver Seniors, we offer a spectrum of wellness and community services.

However, with the recent developments of the Covid-19 pandemic, we are not operating at our full capacity. We have adapted to specific guidelines put in place by the Governor. We cater to a high risk community so we take these precautions very seriously.

Monthly News

National Breast Cancer Awareness Month

We are an organization based in the need to help others. Either our members have been directly battling breast cancer or seen a loved one go through the pain. Click the button to donate today.



Trunk or Treat

The Annual Silver Seniors Trunk or Treat festival is still planning to happen pending Covid guidelines. We ask that all candy be individually wrapped and to wear gloves when passing out candy. If you have any questions, visit the contact page.

2020 Coat Drive

The 2020 Coat Drive is a necessity while temperatures are declining. We ask you dig out those old coats you do not use anymore to help others. There is a coat dropoff bin in the lobby. At the end of each week, the coats will be collected and given to the homeless via St. Vincent de Paul.
